Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 27, 2025

Summary: AIMUSE does not collect, store, or share any personal user data. This service operates as an automated content creation tool and does not track users.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

AIMUSE is designed with privacy in mind. We explicitly do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Email addresses or contact information
  • User behavior or browsing data
  • Cookies or tracking data
  • Device information or IP addresses
  • Location data
  • Analytics or usage statistics

2. How the Service Works

AIMUSE operates as an automated system that:

  • Generates music using AI technology (Suno.ai)
  • Creates video content from generated audio
  • Posts content to social media platforms via official APIs
  • Does not require user accounts or login
  • Does not access or store any user data

3. Third-Party Services

AIMUSE integrates with the following third-party services for content creation and distribution:

Suno.ai

Used for AI music generation. Please review Suno.ai's privacy policy for information about their data practices.

TikTok

Content is posted to TikTok using their official Content Posting API. TikTok's privacy policy governs data handling on their platform.

Instagram (Meta)

Content is posted to Instagram Reels using the Instagram Graph API. Meta's privacy policy applies to content posted on Instagram.

YouTube (Google)

Content is posted to YouTube Shorts using the YouTube Data API. Google's privacy policy governs YouTube data handling.

4. Website Cookies

This website (hosted on GitHub Pages) does not use cookies or any tracking technologies. GitHub may collect basic analytics as described in their privacy policy.

5. Data Security

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal information to secure. API credentials used for posting content are stored securely and encrypted.

6. Children's Privacy

AIMUSE is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Posted Content

Content posted to social media platforms is subject to those platforms' privacy policies:

  • All posted content is public
  • Content is visible according to platform settings
  • Engagement metrics are collected by the platforms, not by AIMUSE
  • Users can interact with posted content according to platform features

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. However, you may:

  • Contact us with questions about this Privacy Policy
  • Request information about our data practices
  • Report any privacy concerns

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. International Data Transfers

Since no personal data is collected, there are no international data transfers of user data. Content is posted to global social media platforms according to their data handling practices.

11. Contact Information

For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository.

12. Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with major privacy regulations including:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

Compliance is straightforward as we do not collect personal data.
